Based in Belgrano, SCK Arquitectos is an architectural studio with over 20 years of experience. Rangeing over unifamiliar houses to buildings, SCK has buildt all over CABA and Gran Buenos Aires.

We believe architecture is more than construction; it is an experience. Our projects aim to inspire through form, texture, and spatial quality, creating places that people connect with on an emotional level. Beyond solving practical needs, we design with the intention of sparking curiosity, encouraging interaction, and fostering a sense of belonging. This vision allows our work to transcend utility, becoming part of the collective memory of the community.

By adapting to our surroundings, we ensure that each project enriches the life of the neighborhood.

Our approach emphasizes long-term value. We carefully select materials and construction methods that stand the test of time, while also allowing flexibility for future needs.
